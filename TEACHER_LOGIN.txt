========================================
HOW TO TAKE ATTENDANCE
========================================

STEP 1: Click "Take Attendance" from dashboard

STEP 2: Select your class from dropdown
- You'll see: P1 A, P2 A, P3 A, P4 A, P5 A, P6 A
- Each shows student count

STEP 3: Select date (defaults to today)

STEP 4: Mark attendance for each student
- Click "Present" (green) - default
- Click "Absent" (red) - student not in class
- Click "Late" (yellow) - student arrived late
- Click "Excused" (blue) - excused absence

STEP 5: Add notes (optional)
- Type reason for absence/lateness
- Example: "Sick", "Doctor appointment", "Family emergency"

STEP 6: Click "Save Attendance"
- Confirmation message appears
- Data saved to database
